## If They Ask For Revisions

- **If they say:** "Your questions are too many" → Simplify to top 3 (compensation, sales process, Bali requirement)
- **If they say:** "We can't disclose previous operator earnings" → Ask for a range or a min/max that covers your floor
- **If they say:** "Just apply normally, we'll discuss later" → Good sign; they're interested. Simplify the proposal and resubmit.
- **If they say:** "Sales closing is 100% your responsibility" → Negotiate harder on base salary or ask for a trial period with lower rate
- **If they say:** "Location is non-negotiable Bali" → Clarify: Do you mean physically present X days/week, or based in the city?
